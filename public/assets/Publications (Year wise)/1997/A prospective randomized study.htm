A prospective randomised study of the role of combination of interferon and radiation therapy in the management of locally advanced carcinoma cervix(stage IIIB)-A preliminary report.
Journal of Clinical Radiotherapy & Oncology. 1997 Jul-Dec; 12(1-2): 28-31
ABSTRACT: Aim:To evaluate the role of interferon,in combination with radiother-apy,in the management of locally advanced carcinoma cervix patients.Materials & methods:Thirty three patients with carcinoma cervix stage IIIB were randomised into two treatment groups-radiotherapy alone group(15 patients)and radiotherapy with interferon group(18 patients)during the period between february 1995 and f-ebruary 1996 at our institution.Teletherapy was given twice a week,using Cobalt unit followed by intracavitary treatment with Selectron HDR in all the patients.
